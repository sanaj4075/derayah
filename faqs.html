What is D360?

A full digital bank in an app made by Saudi people, in Saudi Arabia. To provide all banking services that are elevated with technology to enable banking for everyone in the Kingdom.

Does D360 have a banking license?

D360 has obtained its banking license from the Saudi Arabian Monetary Authority (SAMA).

What features will D360 have?
  • • Open a bank account from wherever you are in KSA.
  • • Send money online faster within the Kingdome, and to any country in the world.
  • • Low transfer fees and cheap FX rates.
  • • Free and instant bank account that you can use online and everywhere.
